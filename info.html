astroXpress is a newly established brand. We are striving for the top position regarding tourism in space, by doing everything we can to make sure every person is going to get to experience space in the near future. We aim to provide the cheapest and most accessible flight for our passengers without compromising security, even going to extreme lengths to proof both our space shuttles, our seating and ordering custom made, specially designed spacesuits.

The astroXpress shuttle engines are revolutionary, traveling at a stunning speed of 17.500 miles per. hr. (28.000 km/h). At this speed, you will be able to see a sunrise or sunset every 45 minutes. To make this possible, we have created our very own patterned chemical fuel composition, consisting of hydrogen, oxygen and a very special compound called quercetin. This enables our shuttles to take off and land with more elegance, making it a more comfortable ride for the passengers.
